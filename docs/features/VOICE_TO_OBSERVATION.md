# Voice-to-Observation

## Speak It. We Structure It.

### The Problem

Lesson observations are essential for school improvement, but:
- **30+ minutes** to write up each observation
- Notes get lost or become illegible
- Feedback delayed (sometimes days)
- Inconsistent format across observers
- No connection to research evidence
- Write-ups pile up, reducing observation frequency

**Result:** Leaders do fewer observations because the admin burden is too high.

### The Solution

Record your observations verbally. Let AI do the structuring.

### What You Get

**1. Summary**
A concise 2-3 sentence overview of the lesson.

**2. Strengths (3-5 specific points)**
- Evidence-based observations
- Linked to good practice
- Specific, not generic

**3. Areas for Development (2-3 points)**
- Constructive and actionable
- Prioritised by impact

**4. Ratings Across 6 Areas**
- Subject Knowledge (1-4)
- Pedagogy (1-4)
- Adaptive Teaching (1-4)
- Assessment (1-4)
- Behaviour Management (1-4)
- Engagement (1-4)

**5. EEF Research Recommendations**
Automatic linking to Education Endowment Foundation strategies:
- "Feedback (+6 months): Consider more specific next-step guidance"
- "Collaborative Learning (+5 months): Increase structured talk"

**6. Suggested Actions**
Specific next steps for the teacher:
- Observe a colleague
- Attend specific CPD
- Try a specific strategy

**7. Ofsted Framework Links**
Automatic tagging to relevant framework areas.

### Time Savings

| Traditional | Voice-to-Observation |
|-------------|---------------------|
| 30 min write-up | 2 min total |
| Delayed feedback | Same-day feedback |
| 3-4 observations/week | 10+ observations/week |
| Inconsistent format | Always consistent |
| No research links | Automatic EEF links |

### Benefits

**For School Leaders:**
- Do more observations with less time
- Consistent, evidence-based feedback
- Automatic framework alignment

**For Teachers:**
- Faster feedback
- Research-backed recommendations
- Clear, actionable next steps

**For School Improvement:**
- More observation data
- Pattern identification across school
- Evidence base for CPD priorities
