| Question | Yes | No |

## ‚öôÔ∏è First-Time Setup Guide

### Step 1: Create Your Organisation
1. Sign in with Google or Microsoft
2. Click "Create New Organisation"
3. Enter your school name and URN
4. Select school type and phase

### Step 2: Invite Your Team
1. Go to Settings ‚Üí Team
2. Click "Invite Member"
3. Enter email address and select role
4. They'll receive an invitation email

### Step 3: Configure Settings
1. Enable/disable Church School features (SIAMS)
2. Set notification preferences
3. Review privacy settings

### Step 4: Start Using
1. Complete your first self-assessment
2. Scan some evidence documents
3. Create your first action
