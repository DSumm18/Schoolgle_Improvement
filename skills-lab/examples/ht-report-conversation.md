## **Tips for Your Report**

1. **Keep it focused**: 4-6 pages main report (you can add data appendices)
2. **Show impact, not just activity**: "CPD delivered and writing up 4%" not just "CPD delivered"
3. **Be honest**: Governors can't help with challenges they don't know about
4. **Link to SIP**: Keep referencing your 3 priorities throughout
5. **Pose questions**: Engage governors in strategic thinking
6. **Use data meaningfully**: Interpret, don't just present numbers
7. **Make decisions clear**: Don't bury "we need approval for X" - make it explicit
